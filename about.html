About

I made this license and generator for myself, because Creative Commons feels like it's not enough personally. I don't push people to think they have to use ADAB, but if they use this, you have my thanks.

The name ADAB came from the Indonesian word adab, which means "kehalusan dan kebaikan budi pekerti, kesopanan, atau akhlak", or refinement and goodness of character, politeness, or morals.
